Quick Notes

Key Principle

Antivirals usually do not destroy viruses directly. They reduce viral multiplication by blocking specific stages in the viral life cycle.

High-yield Drug Examples

  • Amantadine / Rimantadine — uncoating of Influenza A
  • Oseltamivir / Zanamivir — neuraminidase inhibition
  • Ribavirin — inhibits RNA synthesis
  • Acyclovir / Ganciclovir — inhibit viral DNA polymerase
  • Interferon — stimulates host antiviral response
